Viva

Since 2015 Severin Gsponer is a PhD student at the Insight Centre for Data Analytics at the University College Dublin, Ireland. He holds a MSc(2014) and a BSc(2012) in Computer Science.
His current research focus is on sequence regression in particular the analysis of different loss functions and convex optimization strategies within the sequence learning framework SEQL. Thereby, he’s especially interested in the scalability as well as interpretability of the produced models. Additionally he works on improvements of the feature selection strategies in huge feature spaces with structure, like n-gram spaces. Severin Gsponer is also interested in a variety of other Computer Science related topics like: recurrent neural networks, feature learning, multitask learning, transfer learning, bioinformatics, repoducibility in research and exascale computing.
